## Core Responsibilities

As a moderator, you are charged with the following essential duties. Failure to perform these duties may be subject to investigation by SID.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Uphold Rules & Policies" icon="book-bookmark">
    Proactively and reactively enforce all community rules, Valley Prison's rules, Roblox's Community Standards, and Discord's Terms of Service.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="In-Game & Discord Moderation" icon="gamepad">
    Actively moderate official shifts and monitor Discord channels to address rule violations, maintain order, and ensure compliance with our rules.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ticket Management & Appeals" icon="ticket">
    Respond to and resolve user-submitted support tickets professionally. Senior Moderators are also tasked with handling ban appeals with impartiality.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Community Engagement & Directives" icon="users">
    Foster a positive relationship with the community by engaging professionally and executing all lawful directives issued by supervisory staff.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Code of Conduct

Accepting a position in this division means you accept a higher standard of conduct. Your actions, on and off duty, reflect on the entire staff team.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Professionalism & Respect" icon="user-tie">
    Maintain a respectful and calm tone in all interactions. Refrain from using derogatory language and avoid discussing inappropriate (NSFW) topics.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Impartiality & Fairness" icon="scale-balanced">
    All moderation decisions must be based on evidence and established rules, not personal feelings. You **must** recuse yourself from any conflict of interest.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Integrity & Accountability" icon="stamp">
    You are subject to the policies outlined in the **SID Operations Handbook**. Violations such as Command Abuse or Failure to Moderate will be referred to SID.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Confidentiality" icon="file-lock">
    Protecting sensitive information is mandatory. All internal staff communications, user punishment records, and internal documents are considered classified.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Use of Moderation Tools

Moderation commands are powerful tools, not toys. Their use is logged and subject to review. Abuse is a serious offense investigated by SID.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="In-Game Commands">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Soft Ban" description="Removes a player from the current shift server." icon="hammer">
        **Command:** `;softban (username) (reason)`

        Use this command to remove disruptive players from the server. A reason is required.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Discord Commands">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Ban" description="Permanently removes a member from the Discord." icon="hammer">
        **Command:** `/ban (username) (reason)`

        Used for severe or repeated offenses. A reason is required.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Kick" description="Removes a member, allowing them to rejoin." icon="boot">
        **Command:** `/kick (username) (reason)`

        Used to de-escalate a volatile situation. A reason is required.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Mute" description="Revokes a member's ability to speak or chat." icon="volume-xmark">
        **Command:** `/mute (username) (duration) (reason)`

        Used for chat-based offenses. Duration and reason are required.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Warn" description="Issues a formal, logged warning to a member." icon="triangle-exclamation">
        **Command:** `/warn (username) (reason)`

        The first step for most minor rule violations. A reason is required.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Priority" description="Sets the priority level of a ticket." icon="arrow-up-wide-short">
        **Command:** `/priority (low/medium/high)`

        Use within a ticket to set its urgency.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
